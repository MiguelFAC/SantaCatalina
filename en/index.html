About

Know our history..

  • 2013

    From the beginning creating unforgettable moments in the hearts of our clients.

    We are located in Piedades de Santa Ana, on Calle Pilares. We are a company that started on March 1, 2013, the idea first originated with relatives and close people.

  • 2015

    An agency, a Family and your Community.

    When thinking about the potential of the building and its large green areas, we were motivated to hold events for all audiences, with a complete package which would allow them to have everything our clients need..

  • 2017

    We are for you.

    In this way, our clients could enjoy their events in a calm way and with the only concern of enjoying their event..

  • 2020

    Let's celebrate your special event together.

    With almost 10 years of experience, most of the events we have carried out have been weddings, birthdays and family celebrations.

  • Prueba de la linea de tiempo

    Transition to Full Service

    Desde el incio del 2022 hasta finales de setiembre hemos realizado alrededor de 35 eventos en este año. Nuestra misión principal es; “Ayudar a nuestros clientes a disfrutar de un día inolvidable lo mejor posible”

  • 2021

    A good value agency.

    In our experience doing this job, we can affirm that it entails a great amount of discipline, organization, patience, order, serenity and tranquility.

  • 2023

    And in these last few months...

    Our greatest satisfaction comes from seeing our clients enjoying and spending a quality moment with their loved ones in the events that we have helped them create, also knowing that they will remember it for a lifetime.
